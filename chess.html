You turn is highlighted in green. Click to any cell to begin. If the figure is not green, you can't move it

Blue highlight will show where the figure can move. If you touch the figure you have to move it

  • "K" - king - 王 moves to any of it's 8 near cells
  • "B" - bishop - 角 moves diagonally
  • "P" - pawn - 歩 moves only to one cell forward
  • "A" - arrow - 香 only moves forward
  • "N" - knight - 桂 can "jump over" all other pieces, moves to a square that is two squares away horizontally and one square vertically
  • "S" - silver - 銀 moves to any diagonal cell and forward
  • "G" - gold - 金 moves to any cell except the one behind diagonally
